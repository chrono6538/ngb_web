# About Nordic Garden Buildings

Nordic Garden Buildings is a leading supplier of insulated PVC-u garden rooms, garden offices and outdoor living spaces.

For more than 40 years, our group has been manufacturing high-quality home improvement products from factories in the valleys of South Wales. Nordic Garden Buildings is a privately owned business and part of a wider group of established home-improvement brands.

We employ over 200 people across multiple manufacturing sites and work with a network of retail and installation partners.
